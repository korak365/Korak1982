# What are Apify Actors?

Actors are serverless cloud programs that can perform anything from a simple action, like filling out a web form, to a complex operation, like crawling an entire website or monitoring specific data.

This Actor is designed to scrape websites and identify companies using specific high-ticket software, such as Salesforce, HubSpot, or Dynamics 365. It collects data such as the company's name, website, contact info, and detected technologies, which can be used for lead generation and outreach.
